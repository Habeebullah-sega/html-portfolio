My top 5 movies

Jumong

Jumong is a South Korean historical drama television series about the life of Jumong.
the founder of Goguryeo, one of the Three Kingdoms of Korea.
The series aired on MBC from 2006 to 2007 and was a huge success in South Korea and internationally.
It is known for its epic storytelling, strong performances, and high production values.

Game of thrones

Game of Thrones is an American fantasy drama television series created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss for HBO. It is an adaptation of A Song of Ice and Fire, a series of fantasy novels by George R. R. Martin. The show aired from 2011 to 2019 and became a cultural phenomenon, known for its complex characters, intricate political plots, and epic battles.

vikings

Vikings is a historical drama television series created and written by Michael Hirst for the History channel. The show is inspired by the sagas of Viking Ragnar Lothbrok, one of the best-known legendary Norse heroes and notorious as the scourge of England and France. The series aired from 2013 to 2020 and is praised for its historical accuracy, compelling storytelling, and strong performances.
